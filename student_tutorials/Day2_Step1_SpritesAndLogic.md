# Day 2: Sprites and Game Logic

Welcome to Day 2! Today we'll customize characters in our game.

## Step 1: Add a Custom NPC
**File to edit:** `settings.py`
**Search for:** `@STUDENT-EDIT-Day2-1`

Let's add a new Non-Player Character (NPC) to our farm!
**Hint:** Look at the `NPC_DATA` dictionary. You can copy the "Robin" entry and paste it below to create a second character. 
Change the name, adjust the `pos` (position on the screen), and maybe change the `"graphic"` path to point to a different image in your `graphics` folder. Don't forget to update the `dialogue` list too!
